      </div>

      </div>
    </div>
    
    <div className="glass-card p-6 space-y-4">

## Common Prompting Mistakes

<div className="space-y-6">
  <h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-center">What to Avoid</h3>
  
  <div className="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <h4 className="font-bold text-red-600 dark:text-red-400">‚ùå Don't Do This</h4>
      
      <div className="glass-card p-4 border-red-200 dark:border-red-800">
        <h5 className="font-semibold mb-2">Too Vague</h5>
        <p className="text-sm font-mono bg-red-100 dark:bg-red-900/50 p-2 rounded">
          "Help me with my project"
        </p>
        <p className="text-xs text-muted-foreground mt-1">What project? What kind of help?</p>
      </div>
      
      <div className="glass-card p-4 border-red-200 dark:border-red-800">
        <h5 className="font-semibold mb-2">Contradictory Instructions</h5>
        <p className="text-sm font-mono bg-red-100 dark:bg-red-900/50 p-2 rounded">
          "Write a short 5-page essay"
        </p>
        <p className="text-xs text-muted-foreground mt-1">Short or 5 pages? Pick one!</p>
      </div>
      
      <div className="glass-card p-4 border-red-200 dark:border-red-800">
        <h5 className="font-semibold mb-2">Assuming Context</h5>
        <p className="text-sm font-mono bg-red-100 dark:bg-red-900/50 p-2 rounded">
          "Continue from where we left off"
        </p>
        <p className="text-xs text-muted-foreground mt-1">The AI doesn't remember previous conversations</p>
      </div>
    </div>
    
    <div className="space-y-4">
      <h4 className="font-bold text-green-600 dark:text-green-400">‚úÖ Do This Instead</h4>
      
      <div className="glass-card p-4 border-green-200 dark:border-green-800">
        <h5 className="font-semibold mb-2">Be Specific</h5>
        <p className="text-sm font-mono bg-green-100 dark:bg-green-900/50 p-2 rounded">
          "Help me outline a marketing strategy for my food truck business"
        </p>
        <p className="text-xs text-muted-foreground mt-1">Clear task and context</p>
      </div>
      
      <div className="glass-card p-4 border-green-200 dark:border-green-800">
        <h5 className="font-semibold mb-2">Clear Requirements</h5>
        <p className="text-sm font-mono bg-green-100 dark:bg-green-900/50 p-2 rounded">
          "Write a concise 2-page essay"
        </p>
        <p className="text-xs text-muted-foreground mt-1">Consistent and clear</p>
      </div>
      
      <div className="glass-card p-4 border-green-200 dark:border-green-800">
        <h5 className="font-semibold mb-2">Provide Context</h5>
        <p className="text-sm font-mono bg-green-100 dark:bg-green-900/50 p-2 rounded">
